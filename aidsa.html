About Us

Who We Are

The All India Dental Students and Surgeons Association (AIDSA) is the independent and united voice of dental professionals in India. Established in 2021, AIDSA was founded with a clear purpose — to bring together dental students, interns, postgraduates, and practicing dental surgeons under a single national platform. Since its inception, 100+ dental colleges and more than 30,000 dental students across India have been associated with AIDSA. Alongside, 10,000+ dental surgeons have joined the movement, making AIDSA one of the fastest-growing organizations in the dental fraternity. AIDSA is living proof of the power of unity and vision — a dream that started with one student leader and now represents thousands of voices from every corner of India.

Founded in 2010, we have grown to represent over 10,000 dental students from more than 100 institutions nationwide.

Our Mission

AIDSA is dedicated to empowering dental students and surgeons by enhancing their skills, upgrading their knowledge, and protecting their professional interests. Our mission includes: 1. Supporting scientific innovation to meet the changing needs of society. 2. Promoting research, education, and training in dentistry. 3. Creating awareness and preventive campaigns against oral diseases and tobacco-related cancers. 4. Ensuring the timely transfer of knowledge from research to practice and policy-making. 5. Conducting Continuing Dental Education (CDE) and professional development programs to build capable leaders of tomorrow. 6. Standing as a guardian for dental rights, ensuring fair opportunities, policies, and dignity for the profession.

Our Vision

The vision of AIDSA is: To improve oral health and quality of life across India. To ensure that every dental student grows into a confident professional with global exposure. To build a strong professional community that stands united for rights, recognition, and reforms. To lead national campaigns against oral cancer, quackery, and unemployment in dentistry. Through future collaborations with international bodies like ASDA (American Student Dental Association), EDSA (European Dental Students Association), ASPDSA (Asia Pacific Dental Students Association) and others, AIDSA envisions a strong international platform for Indian dentists.

Our President

Dr.MD Manzur Ahmed

National& Founder President, All India Dental Students and Surgeons Association

Dr.MD Manzur Ahmed, is a distinguished dental professional with over 15 years of experience in the field. He completed his BDS from Maulana Azad Institute of Dental Sciences and MDS in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery from Manipal College of Dental Sciences.

Under his leadership, AIDSA has expanded its reach to over 150 dental colleges across India and launched several initiatives to support dental students in their academic and professional journeys.

Dear Dental Fraternity, It is my proud privilege to lead the All India Dental Students and Surgeons Association (AIDSA) — the united and independent voice of every dental student and surgeon in our nation. AIDSA is not just an association; it is a movement of courage, unity, and change. We are committed to protecting the dignity of our profession, fighting against injustice, and ensuring that dentistry receives the recognition it truly deserves in India’s healthcare system. From securing student rights and resolving institutional challenges to advocating for national policy reforms and standing firmly against quackery, AIDSA has proven that when dentists unite, we are a force impossible to ignore. I call upon every dental professional to rise beyond boundaries of college, state, or designation — and join this movement. Together, let us shape a future where every dentist finds opportunity, every student finds guidance, and every citizen of India finds access to quality oral healthcare. United we stand, stronger we grow — this is the spirit of AIDSA.
